Siegwerk Track & Trace

Designing an online track and trace process for bulk ink shipments

Summary

Siegwerk customer portal is a B2B e-commerce portal for Siegwerk customers who buy raw material ink in bulk. The portal is used by customers all around the world. Customers are large companies who do commercialised printing. E.g printing houses who print for brands such as Cocacola

Role

Sole UX / UI designer

The Challenge

The challenge of this project was to give customers the ability to track their ink purchase after its done on customer portal.

Period

March 2019 - June 2019

Constraints

I encountered the following constraints while trying to design a solution:

  • Germany business units do not have a single logistics provider that can provide tracking data, hence solution couldn’t be a singular approach
  • China only access the portal via wechat whereas other users access via desktop browsers
  • Because of language barriers, customer representatives have to be consulted and test with instead of end users

Process

To design this solution, I went through a series of steps to get the job done:

  1. Disovery:
    • Multiple sessions with product owner to understand business requirements and perspective
    • Workshop with China customer representatives to understand user needs
    • Workshop with SAP team to understand how tracking data were stored regionally and across the globe for Siegwerk customers
  2. Ideation: Sketching possible solutions and testing with customer representatives, who in turn test with customers in their regions.
  3. Iterating sketches based on different requirements
  4. Hi-fi design and prototyping using the Figma design tool
  5. Feedback workshop explaining design solutions to stakeholders, developers and customer reps
  6. Final iteration and hand off to developers

    7. Outcome

    The following results were achieved due to the previously mentioned process:

    • We decided to split tracking methodologies for customers due to technical constraints. Customers in regions where logistics provider were available get real time tracking of their deliveries. While customers in other regions get delivery statuses such as “In transit”“ or “delayed”.
    • Since Chinese customers mostly use mobile or access via wechat, I emphasized on mobile accuracy.
    • These features are being used by Siegwerk customers now on the portal
