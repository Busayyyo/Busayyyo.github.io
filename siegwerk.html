Siegwerk Digital

Efforts at digitalising a 150 year company processes

Note: Details of work is protected by NDA. I'm happy to provide details during an interview or forward as a pdf file

Summary: Siegwerk is a chemical company and one of the largest suppliers of raw material ink in the world. Siegwerk digital involves digitalising the 150 year old company's customer services and internal processes. I'm part of the young design team working on the digital innovation.

Role: User experience designer

Period: February - November 2019

Responsibilties: My Responsibilties at Siegwerk include but not limited to:

  • Working closely with product owners on vision and product roadmap
  • Employing the HCD process and methods to solve business problems
  • Facilitating design sprint and workshops
  • Consulting on multiple teams within the company on usability and user experience
  • Conducting user research for internal projects
  • Refining products based on new business requirements & user insights
  • Visual design of user interface
  • Communicating design solutions to developers and stakeholders
  • Utilizing 'storytelling' to help teams sell their ideas to stakeholders
  • Creation of design system
