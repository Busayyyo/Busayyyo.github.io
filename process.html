How I work

Design Process

As a student of usability engineering, I'm trained to look beyond the surface. To ask questions such as "who, what, why, where, when and how". Design is a combination of tested and validated decisions and it's best when the user is centered all through the process, listening to their concerns, their thoughts and iterating the process with insights learnt.

I've also found communicating with the team and stakeholders throughout the process, a vital key to an efficient and effective work. As a user centered designer, I consider myself an advocate of users within a team.

While each project differs and have its own requirements, I've found this process to be a guiding map into doing great UX work.
