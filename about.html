About me

UX specialist with background in psychology, storytelling and frontend development

Hi there, my name is Busayo Oyewole and I'm a user experience designer, who is rounding up her masters degree programme in Usability Engineering.

Like all good designers, I love problem solving, with no restriction on the industry. Is there a problem to solve, or just looking for a better way to do things, I'm in.

I'm passionate about how people use technology and how to improve on that experience.

I also enjoy writing UI code and seeing visual designs come to life.

Outside of design, I write fictional stories with hopes of publishing a novel someday. I enjoy poetry and in my free time I serve as the managing editor for Millennial Poets - a Medium poetry publication.
