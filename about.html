Who am I?

Hi there! I'm Busayo Oyewole, a grad student studying usability engineering and building a career in product development.

Like all people who wear many hats, I identify to all sorts of titles:

(i) human centered designer: because I care about the why and how people do things and how to help solve their real problems with technology. I believe that the difference between great and mediocre products is listening to people and empathising with them. Asking questions such as: "where does it hurt?" and "how can I help?" can make a difference, so I ask it often, in a non-cheesy way, and employ the user centered design methodology to provide and implement solutions

(ii) developer: because I solve problems with client facing technologies such as html, css, javascript and react.js.

When I'm not researching an idea or developing one, I enjoy reading and writing fiction :)
