Vendors.so

Designing an ecommerce platform for independent sellers in Nigeria

Summary

Vendors is a new startup in Nigeria that helps independent sellers create an online shop, process orders and payment. In this project, I helped with product thinking from conception to UX flows to implementation.

Role

Product designer

The Challenge

A platform for independent sellers who mostly sell on instagram via dms to set up an online shop easily and start selling.

Project period

November 2020

Process

To answer the question of how to make this process digitally transparent, it involved understanding how ink production and distribution works, peculiarities to country to country management and distribution of ink, established processes for ink management within Instaff and cross cultural management with account managers across continents to get this feature designed and implemented.

  1. Discovery:
    • Interviews with independent sellers who mostly sell via Instagram
    • Analyzing data gotten from interviews and mapping needs with business objectives
  2. Rapid prototyping: Using typeforms to simulate how an online shop could be for independent sellers and testing with sellers.
  3. Iteration Based on feedback gotten from the rapid prototype, I iterated on the typeform version, but it was still limited to serve the user needs.
  4. Hi-fi design and prototyping using Figma, I designed a hifi design of what the product should look like, for both the sellers and their customers.

Outcome

  • While the sellers are the primary users of the platform, used for setting up their products, I also have to design for the end customers in mind.
  • Vendors.so is live now on vendors.so
